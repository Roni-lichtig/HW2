Throne of Glass vs. A Court of Thorns and Roses

Throne of Glass by Sarah J. Maas is a thrilling fantasy series following the story of Celaena Sardothien, a skilled assassin in a corrupt kingdom. Her journey is filled with magic, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, making it a captivating read.

A Court of Thorns and Roses (ACOTAR), also by Sarah J. Maas, takes readers into the world of Fae, exploring themes of love, power, and sacrifice. The protagonist, Feyre, embarks on a dangerous journey that intertwines her fate with the Fae realms.
